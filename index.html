Overview Visualization

Overview

P38 MAP kinase is a signaling protein that helps cells respond to stress and inflammation, and it is a well studied drug target. In this demo we visualize the crystal ligand from PDB 3FLY (plus a refined pose from SILCS-MC) bound in the active site to give a concrete reference for how small molecules interact with the pocket.

SILCS FragMaps are 3D grids that summarize where different functional groups are most favorable around the protein; they act like probability and free-energy maps for potential binding hot spots. Overlaying FragMaps with ligands helps interpret why a pose is favorable and where alternative interactions might be optimized. Use the visualization page to toggle FragMap surfaces, switch between ligand poses, and adjust contour levels to explore the binding landscape.
